# Creating a Team

<Divider />

Builder allows you to create and manage teams dedicated to specific projects.

1. <div className="flex flex-col gap-2">Log in to your Builder account. <span className="text-neutral-400"> Ensure you are logged into your Builder account. If you don't have an account yet, <a href="/onboarding" className="hover:text-white underline underline-offset-2">sign up</a> for one to access the team management features. </span></div>

2. Navigate to the Applications page.

3. Click the **Create Team** button.
