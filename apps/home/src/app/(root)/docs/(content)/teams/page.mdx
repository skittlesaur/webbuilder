# Teams

<Divider />

Builder allows you to create and manage teams dedicated to specific projects.

## Create a Team

1. Log in to your Builder account.

<div className="ml-8 text-neutral-400">
  Ensure you are logged into your Builder account. If you don't have an account
  yet, sign up for one to access the team management features.
</div>

2. Navigate to the [Application Dashboard](/app).

3. Click on the Plus icon in the left panel or Create a new team.
